Polytopes

You likely know what a polygon is but probably not a polyhedron, polychoron, etc. Put simply polygons(2d) are shapes made of lines, polyhedra(3d) are shapes made of polygons, and polychora(4d) are just shapes made of polyhedra. So polytopes is just the word used to refer to shapes regardless of the dimension.

Insects

People are often aware of how spiders aren't insects "because they have 8 legs" but that reason is more so a side effect of the simple fact that they come from different evolutionary lines. The difference is better understood when you understand taxonomy.
